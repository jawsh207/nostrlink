About NostrLink


NostrLink is a list of open source projects* aimed to help make getting started with Nostr easier. It's not intended to be a database of all available resources but I do want to provide a bit of everything. If you have any recommendations or you are the author of any software you think would make a good addition, contact me on Nostr via the npub below to discuss having it added.

Requirements
There are not many. Anything added to this list must meet only a few requirements. It must be open source*, it must be mostly stable and it must be actively developed.

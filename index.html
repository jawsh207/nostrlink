Lightning Wallets

Wallets store and manage your funds on the Lightning Network, a payment protocol layered on top of Bitcoin which allows users to send or receive Bitcoin quickly and cheaply.

  • Alby - a wallet provider & more visit

Relay Software

Relays act like servers for Nostr. Clients connect to them to send events, which are then relayed to other clients and/or relays. Relays can be public or require payment to use. Anybody can run a relay.

  • Mostr - a bridge between Nostr & Fediverse source
  • nostream - a relay written in TypeScript source
  • s0str - a private Nostr relay source
